# Privacy

Home Bridge Organizer / 家桥整理器 is local-first.

- No analytics.
- No cloud service.
- No network calls from the app.
- No accessory, room, home, or bridge data is uploaded.
- Room maps are local JSON files selected by the user.
- Apple Home access is requested only through Apple's HomeKit permission prompt.

If you share logs or room maps publicly, redact home names, accessory names, entity IDs, and any other details you consider private.
